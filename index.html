Specifications

Seismic-resistant RCC structure using system formwork with concrete walls in main building & RCC framed structure up to ground floor and amenity block with block work and plastering.

DOORS: ENGINEERED WOODEN DOORS

Main door: Engineered wooden veneer finish.
Bedrooms & toilet doors: Engineered wooden doors with laminate finish.

FLOORING AND WALL CLADDING

Living, dining, kitchen & utility: Vitrified tiles.
All bedrooms: Laminated wooden flooring.
Balcony & bathroom: Vitrified or ceramic tiles.

INTERNAL WALL FINISH

Internal walls & ceiling: Acrylic emulsion.

BALCONY RAILINGS

Balcony: RCC parapet with MS top rail / MS railings with glass as per façade design.

WINDOWS, SLIDING DOORS & VENTILATORS

3-track UPVC wooden-finish laminated profile.
Windows and sliding door with mosquito mesh.
Bathrooms: Powder-coated aluminium ventilators with etched glass profile.

FALSE CEILING

Living balcony, master bedroom balcony & toilets: Wooden-finish PVC panel false ceiling.

EXTERNAL WALL FINISH

External walls: Combination of cladding and texture-finish with exterior grade emulsion.

CCP AND SANITARY FITTINGS & FIXTURES

Jaguar and Kohler or equivalent water-efficient CP & sanitary fixtures.

GRID POWER AND BACKUP POWER

Power: 3BHK – 5kW.
DG Power Backup: 50% of EB load for inside the apartments & 100% backup for common area lighting, lifts and utilities.

SERVICES

Water Treatment Plant.
Sewage Treatment Plant.

ELEVATORS

2 passenger lifts & one service lift per core of reputed make.

SAFETY AND SECURITY

CCTV coverage on all main entry and exit points.

WATER CONSERVATION

Dual piping system.
Dual flush system for sanitary.
Recycled water used for landscape maintenance.
Water-efficient fixtures.
Rainwater harvesting.
Ground water recharge.

ENERGY CONSERVATION

Provision for solar heated water in one toilet of top 2 floor apartments.
Energy-efficient lighting in common areas.
Energy-efficient streetlights with timer control.
EV charging stations provided in designated areas.

SOLID WASTE MANAGEMENT

Segregation at source.
Organic waste converter.
